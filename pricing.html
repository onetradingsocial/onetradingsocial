Questions

Pricing, answered.

Q.Is it really free during beta?

Yes. Every plan is free while we're in beta. Build your profile, journal your trades, and lock in founding-member pricing before launch — no card required to start.

Q.Can I change or cancel plans anytime?

Upgrade, downgrade or cancel whenever you like. Changes take effect immediately, and your journal history and profile stay exactly where you left them.

Q.What happens to my data if I downgrade?

Nothing is deleted. Trades beyond the Free limit are simply hidden until you upgrade again — your full history is always preserved.

Q.How does annual billing save 2 months?

Choosing annual bills you for ten months instead of twelve — so Trader works out to $25/mo and Pro to $42/mo, paid once a year.

Q.Do you reward bigger trades or leverage?

Never. XP and rank come from discipline — journaling, reviewing losses and finishing lessons. No plan rewards gambling, more size or chasing profit.

Q.Is TradingSocial financial advice?

No. It's an education and performance-tracking platform. We don't provide financial advice. Trading involves risk, and past performance never guarantees future results.
